About Brooklyn College

Brooklyn College is a senior college of the City University of New York, located in Brooklyn, New York. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in various disciplines.

About Our Mission

At our group, we're dedicated to assisting Brooklyn College students in navigating the culinary landscape, ensuring they find delicious and satisfying meals that align with their dietary needs and restrictions. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform where students can easily discover food options tailored to their individual dietary preferences, whether they're vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or have other specific requirements. By empowering students with the resources they need to make informed dining choices, we aim to foster a healthier and more inclusive campus community..
